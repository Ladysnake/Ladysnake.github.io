 Illuminations


 Curseforge Page

What is Illuminations?

Illuminations adds fireflies, glowing insects and other light orbs to the world in order to improve the ambience and liveliness of your world both at night and in low light areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this mod server side or client side?

Since you can interact with fireflies and other entities in this mod, it's not client side only, meaning you need both the server and the client to have the mod installed in order to play with it.

Can I include this mod in a modpack?

You can include this mod in any modpack, private or public, without asking permission, as long as you don't make profit out of it.
